About OwlJudge

OwlJudge is a revolutionary web-based platform designed to streamline student project evaluations. Our mission is to provide a fair, transparent, and efficient process for judges and administrators.

We leverage modern technology to create a seamless experience in academic reviews. Whether you are a judge or an admin, our platform offers innovative tools to manage the entire evaluation process.

Join us as we redefine academic excellence and promote fairness in evaluations.
